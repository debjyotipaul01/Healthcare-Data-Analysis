# 🏥 Healthcare Data Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive **Healthcare Data Analysis Dashboard** developed using **Power BI**. The dashboard provides valuable insights into hospital performance, patient outcomes, revenue generation, departmental efficiency, and healthcare procedures.

The objective of this project is to help healthcare organizations make **data-driven decisions** by analyzing patient records, treatment costs, emergency response times, and departmental performance metrics.

---

## 🎯 Key Objectives

* Analyze overall hospital revenue and patient statistics
* Monitor patient outcomes and readmission rates
* Track department-wise revenue generation
* Identify high-cost medical conditions and procedures
* Evaluate emergency room response efficiency
* Improve healthcare operational decision-making through visualization

---

* **Total Revenue:** 8M
* **Total Patients:** 984
* **Average Rating:** 3.60

### 🔹 Patient Outcome Analysis

* **Recovered:** 60.06%
* **Stable:** 39.94%

This analysis helps hospitals understand treatment effectiveness and patient recovery trends.

---

### 🔹 Readmission Analysis

* **No Readmission:** 73.17%
* **Readmission:** 26.83%

Readmission tracking helps identify areas requiring improved patient care and follow-up procedures.

---

### 🔹 Revenue by Department

The dashboard analyzes revenue generated from different medical procedures such as:

* Surgery & Chemotherapy
* Radiation Therapy
* Cardiac Catheterization
* Angioplasty
* CT Scan & Medication
* Delivery & Postnatal Care

This enables healthcare administrators to identify high-performing departments and allocate resources effectively.

---

### 🔹 Cost Analysis by Medical Condition

The project evaluates treatment costs for various conditions including:

* Cancer
* Heart Disease
* Stroke
* Diabetes
* Hypertension
* Respiratory Disorders
* Fractures
* Kidney Conditions

This analysis helps in understanding healthcare expenditure patterns.

## 📈 Insights Generated

* Surgery and chemotherapy generated the highest revenue.
* Cancer treatment had the highest treatment cost among all conditions.
* Most patients recovered successfully, indicating effective healthcare services.
* Readmission rates highlight opportunities for improving post-treatment care.
* Department-wise seat occupancy analysis helped evaluate operational efficiency.
